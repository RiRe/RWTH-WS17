Information

We will not use L2P, but the [Exercise-System] designed by LuFGI2. If you want to take the exam, then you must (beside registering at the ZPA; see "Modul-Anmeldung") do the following:
  • Register for the Exercise-System (see Uebung0.pdf) by Fr 13.10, 23:59.
  • Reach at least 50% of the points of the homework assignments
  • (Zulassungen aus dem WS 16/17 sind nicht mehr gültig.)
You will be assigned to 1 of 20 small tutorial groups, which will meet weekly. You will be able to submit your preferences on tutorial time slots and groups via the exercise system.
The tutorials will prepare you for the homework assignments. Homework (of groups of size 2) shall be submitted at the box at i1 (Informatik-Zentrum, Erweiterungsbau 1, ground floor) each week before Tuesday/Dienstag 16:15 o'clock:
