Information on homework and tutorials

We will not use the L2P system, but the [Exercise-System] designed by LuFGI2. If you intend to take the exam, then you should (beside registering at the ZPA; see "Modul-Anmeldung") also do the following:
  • Register for the Exercise-System (see Uebung0.pdf); registration is open till Fr 13.10, 23:59.
  • Reach at least 50% of the points on the homework assignments
  • (Zulassungen aus dem WS 16/17 sind nicht mehr gültig.)
You will be assigned to one of 20 small tutorial groups, which meet weekly. You will be able to submit your preferences on tutorial time slots and groups via the exercise system.
The tutorials will prepare you for the homework assignments. Homework (of groups of size 2) shall be submitted at the box at i1 (Informatik-Zentrum, Erweiterungsbau E1, ground floor) every week by Tuesday/Dienstag afternoon, 16:15 o'clock.



Solutions to the homework assignments will be presented in the upcoming Globalübung (GÜ).
